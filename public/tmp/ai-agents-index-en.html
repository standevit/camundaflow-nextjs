AI Agents

An autonomous, intelligent software component that performs tasks, makes decisions and interacts with people and systems — powered by large language models (LLMs) and orchestrated by Camunda 8.

What does an AI agent do?

  • Understands natural language customer requests (email, chat, ticket, voice)
  • Decides which process to start (intent detection)
  • Acts: invokes services, writes to CRM/ERP, creates tickets, schedules appointments
  • Asks for clarification when uncertain (instead of acting incorrectly)
  • Learns from feedback and historical cases (if desired)
  • Remains controllable — every step is traceable in Camunda

Typical use cases (2025/2026)

  • Intelligent customer service (automated email & chat)
  • Claims handling in insurance
  • Complaint management in e-commerce
  • Employee or customer onboarding
  • IT service desk (ticket classification + self-healing)

Why Camunda 8 + AI Agents?

  • 100% transparency — every agent step is a BPMN process
  • Reduced hallucinations through clear rules and DMN decisions
  • Easy integration with OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral, Llama 3, Gemini …
  • Human-in-the-loop when needed (Tasklist escalation)
  • Scales to millions of interactions per day (Zeebe)

Summary: An AI agent is more than a chatbot — it is an orchestration-capable, reliable worker under full process control.
